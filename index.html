Online PassPapers

Online Pass Paper on INFOTECH is a unique feature that allows students to access previous years' exam papers and practice tests online. This resource is invaluable for exam preparation, providing students with the opportunity to familiarize themselves with the format, types of questions, and difficulty level of actual exams. By practicing with these papers, students can assess their knowledge, identify areas for improvement, and build confidence before their exams.
